Waterproofing FAQs

Does this include mold removal?

We scrub and treat accessible surface mold on masonry. If the mold is behind drywall, we recommend a remediation specialist first.

Do I need a French Drain?

If you have puddles on your floor after heavy rain, yes. Sealing the walls won't stop water coming up from the ground pressure.

How messy is it?

Jackhammering concrete creates dust. We use heavy plastic containment zones and exhaust fans to keep the rest of your house clean.
