Window FAQs

Will my house be open to the weather?

No. We replace one window at a time. The opening is exposed for usually less than 15 minutes.

Do you offer triple-pane glass?

Yes, for Signature tiers. However, for most NYC climates, high-quality double-pane with Low-E coating is the best value.

Does the price include the window?

Our "Premium" packages typically include the window unit. For "Signature" or custom doors, you pay for the unit, and we charge for installation.
