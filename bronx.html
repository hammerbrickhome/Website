Built for Bronx Terrain

The Bronx is known for its elevation changes and rocky terrain. Standard masonry often fails here. We use reinforced concrete and deep footings to ensure your walls and steps last for decades.

  • Retaining Walls: Reinforced concrete or stone walls to hold back soil on sloped properties.
  • Sidewalk Repair: We remove DOT violations, handle permits, and pour 4500 PSI concrete.
  • Front Steps: Granite, limestone, or brick steps designed for high foot traffic.
  • Waterproofing: Solving basement leaks common in older Bronx foundations.
